In this video, Fran Allen talks at the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing, in 2006. Worth noting is that her career pre-dates the discipline of computer science! Fran also touches on diversity in hiring (in 1955) and her early work teaching FORTRAN to scientists who had previously been using Assembly Language.
