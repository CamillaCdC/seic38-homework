Who is Jenn Schiffer?

Biography

Jen Schiffer is a full-stack developer, artist and electronic mail writer in jersey city and director of community engineering at glitch. her visual art has been described as "neon abstract pixel erotica" & her web development work falls under the categories of creative coding and comedy

Acomplishments
